Biography

Captain Alena Halves enlisted in the German Army in July 2004 as an officer candidate. She completed her officer development training in Dresden and was commissioned as a Leutnant in 2007.

She has served in various leadership and staff roles including Platoon Leader, Company Executive Officer, Battalion Logistics Officer (S4), and Divisional Staff Officer. Her experience includes operational sustainment planning, readiness oversight, and multinational coordination within NATO frameworks.

Captain Halves deployed to Canada in support of joint training operations with the Canadian Armed Forces, focusing on cold-weather sustainment operations and multinational logistics integration.
